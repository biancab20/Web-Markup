ProefPark

You have to be in the monumental Kenaupark for the late summer festival the last weekend before schools start again. An event with food trucks and their cooking artists in the leading role. With a food line-up of 30 moving kitchens on wheels that together prepare the best street food that is currently available in the Netherlands. All continents are represented: from Asia to Africa and from Europe to South America. Gluten- and lactose-free, vegan, short-seared red meat, desserts and appetizers; you name it and it is offered with over 90 different tastings.

Walking Dinner

Waking Dinner, a night out where you will visit three different restaurants and discover a city in a unique way. A Walking Dinner is the perfect opportunity for tourists to view the highlights of a city while enjoying a delicious 3-course dinner. It’s Walking Dinner’s mission to offer a unique new way of dining whilst in the meantime discovering a city. Walking Dinner has the expertise in setting up the most beautiful city walks. Walking Dinner only cooperates with carefully selected restaurants that offer a great price-quality level. In this way we can offer you a great, culinary and innovative night out for a great value for money.

Dinner Train

Why do the same thing when it can be done differently? Offering dinner is widely known. Traveling by train too, everyone knows the yellow blue trains of NS in the Netherlands. Dining on a moving train? Why not! A special experience with recognizable elements, that's how you could describe Dinner Train. We want to offer our guests something they have not experienced before, but still keep it accessible so that everyone can use it.
